I'm a senior at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science and Practice of Art. This past summer, I was a Design Intern at Apple, where I worked on web-based applications and communications for the developer community. In the past, I've been fortunate enough to work at Google ATAP and Highway Twenty. I'm always looking for opportunities to grow as a designer, so feel free to drop me a line.

Aside from design, I have an undying love for ice skating, printmaking, and sportswear. Finland is my homeland, copper(II) ion is my favorite color, and I wouldn't mind eating pickled vegetables for all my meals.
